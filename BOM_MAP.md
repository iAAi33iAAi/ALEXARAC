## 1. EarthDome Construction

**Foundation:** Polypropylene sandbags (UV stabilized), barbed wire (galvanized), local earth fill, gravel drainage, concrete bond beam, rebar, waterproofing membrane

**Exterior:** Plaster/stucco (lime or cement), waterproof coating, drainage mat, flashing

**Interior:** Earthen/lime/gypsum plaster, low VOC paint, flooring (concrete/tile/earthen)

**Doors/Windows:** Steel or wood entry door, double-pane insulated glass, frames, hardware

**Electrical:** Panel (NEC 2023), Romex NM-B 12/14 AWG, GFCI outlets, LED fixtures, conduit, ground rod

**Solar:** Monocrystalline panels, MPPT controller, pure sine inverter, LiFePO4 battery bank, mounting hardware, disconnects

**Plumbing:** PEX supply, PVC DWV Schedule 40, water heater, fixtures, shutoffs, whole-house filter

**Rainwater:** Catchment coating, gutters/downspouts, first-flush diverter, storage tank, pump, UV treatment

**Smart Home:** Zigbee/Z-Wave hub, smart thermostat, door/window sensors, motion sensors, smart plugs

## 2. LED Amphitheater

**LED Array:** WS2812B/SK6812 strips (IP65), pixel nodes, panels (P2.5-P10), diffusion acrylic, aluminum channels

**Control:** Artnet/sACN controller, DMX splitter, media server, Gigabit switch, Cat6 cabling

**Power:** Mean Well PSUs (5V/12V), power injection cables, fuse blocks, distribution board

**Audio:** Full-range speakers, subwoofer, Class D amp, spatial audio mixer, OFC speaker wire, mounting hardware

**Structure:** Aluminum/steel truss, base plates, cable management, weather covers

## 3. 369 Vortex Water Wheel Cascade

**Wheels (x3):** Marine-grade steel/aluminum frame, 6 HDPE paddles each, hardened steel axle, sealed bearings, mounting brackets

**Cascade (9 stages):** Concrete/HDPE channels, adjustable weirs, vortex chambers, intake screen, tailrace channel

**Generator:** Permanent magnet generator (low RPM per wheel), shaft coupling, rectifier, charge controller, LiFePO4 battery bank, pure sine inverter

**Civil:** Reinforced concrete pads, water intake structure, HDPE/PVC piping, gate valves, overflow spillway

**Monitoring:** Flow sensors per stage, RPM sensors per wheel, power meters, water level sensors, data logger to Aethel Grid

## 4. GRAPALACLAWZ Robotics (per unit)

**Chassis:** Welded steel/aluminum frame, brushless DC motors, motor controllers, wheels/tracks, LiFePO4 battery, charging port

**Sensors:** LiDAR, stereo/depth camera, ultrasonic proximity (4-8), IMU, RTK GPS, bumper contact sensors

**Safety (OSHA):** E-stop button, backup beeper (87dB+), amber strobes, proximity shutdown zone, manual override, reflective markings

**Role Tooling:** Construction: hydraulic arm, bucket, blade. Hauling: flatbed, load sensors. Welding: MIG/TIG torch, wire feeder. Survey: total station, GPS antenna. Planting: seed hopper, auger. Security: spotlight, night vision, speaker.
