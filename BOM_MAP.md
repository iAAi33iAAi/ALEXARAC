## 3. 369 Vortex Water Wheel Cascade

**Wheels (x3):** Marine-grade steel/aluminum frame, 6 HDPE paddles each, hardened steel axle, sealed bearings (2/wheel), mounting brackets

**Monitoring:** Flow sensors (per stage), RPM sensors (per wheel), power meters, water level sensors, data logger (feeds Aethel Grid)
