## 1. EarthDome Construction System

### Foundation and Structure

* Polypropylene sandbags (continuous tube form, UV stabilized)
* Four-point barbed wire (galvanized steel, between each course)
* Local earth fill (on-site soil, sand, clay mix tested for composition)
* Gravel drainage base (crushed stone, French drain perimeter)
* Concrete bond beam (reinforced, top ring for dome compression cap)
* Rebar (for bond beam and any code-required reinforcement)
* Waterproofing membrane (foundation wrap, below grade)

### Exterior Envelope

* Exterior plaster/stucco (lime-based or cement, UV and weather resistant)
* Waterproof coating (elastomeric paint or bitumen layer)
* Drainage mat (behind-grade wall sections)
* Flashing (galvanized steel at window and door penetrations)

### Interior Finish

* Interior plaster (earthen plaster, lime plaster, or gypsum)
* Finish paint or sealant (low VOC, interior grade)
* Flooring (polished concrete, tile, or earthen floor sealant)

### Doors and Windows

* Entry door (steel or solid wood, weatherstripped)
* Window units (double-pane insulated glass, various sizes per tier)
* Window and door frames (pressure-treated lumber or steel)
* Hardware (hinges, locks, handles)

### Electrical System

* Main electrical panel (breaker box, sized per NEC 2023)
* Romex wiring (NM-B cable, 12 AWG and 14 AWG runs)
* Outlets and switches (standard duplex receptacles, GFCI in wet areas)
* LED light fixtures (interior ceiling and wall mount)
* Conduit (where code requires, EMT or PVC)
* Ground rod and grounding wire

### Solar Power System

* Solar panels (monocrystalline, wattage TBD per tier)
* Charge controller (MPPT type)
* Inverter (pure sine wave, grid-tie or off-grid per config)
* Battery bank (lithium iron phosphate or AGM, capacity TBD)
* Mounting hardware (roof or ground mount racking)
* Disconnect switches and combiner box
* Wiring (PV wire, properly gauged)

### Plumbing and Water

* PEX water supply lines (hot and cold runs)
* PVC drain/waste/vent pipe (Schedule 40)
* Water heater (tankless or tank, electric or solar thermal)
* Fixtures (sink, toilet, shower per unit)
* Shutoff valves (at each fixture and main)
* Water filtration unit (whole-house sediment and carbon)

### Rainwater Collection

* Roof catchment surface (dome exterior, food-safe coating if potable)
* Gutters and downspouts (half-round aluminum or PVC)
* First-flush diverter
* Storage tank (polyethylene or ferrocement, capacity TBD)
* Pump (submersible or booster, if needed)
* Filtration and UV treatment (if potable use)

### Smart Home System

* Smart hub controller (Zigbee/Z-Wave compatible)
* Smart thermostat
* Door/window sensors
* Motion sensors
* Smart plugs and switches

## 2. LED Amphitheater System

### LED Array

* Addressable LED strip (WS2812B or SK6812, IP65 rated for outdoor)
* LED pixel nodes (12mm or 20mm, for 3D spatial arrays)
* LED panels (P2.5 or P3 indoor, P5 or P10 outdoor, per tier)
* Diffusion material (frosted acrylic or silicone covers)
* Mounting channels (aluminum extrusion with clips)

### Control System

* LED controller (Artnet/sACN compatible, universe capacity per tier)
* DMX splitter/amplifier (for signal distribution)
* Media server or control PC (running mapping software)
* Network switch (Gigabit, for Artnet distribution)
* Ethernet cabling (Cat6, shielded runs to controllers)

### Power Distribution

* Power supplies (5V or 12V, mean well or equivalent, sized per LED count)
* Power injection cables (to prevent voltage drop across long runs)
* Fuse blocks or breakers (per power zone)
* Power distribution board

### Audio Integration

* Speakers (full range, quantity and wattage per tier)
* Subwoofer (for low frequency, per tier)
* Amplifier (class D, channels per tier)
* Audio interface/mixer (with spatial audio output capability)
* Speaker wire (OFC copper, gauged for run length)
* Speaker mounting hardware (brackets, poles, rigging)

### Structure

* Truss or frame (aluminum or steel, for overhead LED mounting)
* Ground stakes or base plates (for outdoor setups)
* Cable management (raceways, cable ties, velcro wraps)
* Weather covers (if outdoor, UV and rain protection)
